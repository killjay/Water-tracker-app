# Privacy Policy for H2O Water Tracker

**Last Updated:** February 3, 2026

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how H2O Water Tracker ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application. By using H2O Water Tracker, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

When you use our app, we collect the following personal information:

- **Email Address**: Collected when you sign in with Google to create and manage your account
- **Display Name**: Optionally collected from your Google account profile
- **Profile Photo**: Optionally collected from your Google account profile

### Health & Fitness Data

- **Water Intake Records**: Date, time, and amount of water consumed
- **Daily Goals**: Your personalized daily water intake targets
- **Weight Information**: Used to calculate recommended water intake goals (if provided)
- **Activity Level**: Used to personalize hydration recommendations (if provided)
- **Sleep Schedule**: Used to optimize reminder timing (if provided)

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Device Information**: Device model, operating system version
- **App Usage Data**: Features used, session duration, app interactions (via Firebase Analytics)
- **Crash Reports**: Technical information about app errors to improve stability
- **Push Notification Tokens**: Device tokens for sending hydration reminders (if enabled)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### App Functionality
- Create and manage your user account
- Store and sync your water intake data across devices
- Calculate personalized hydration goals
- Display your water intake history and statistics
- Send hydration reminder notifications (if enabled)

### App Improvement
- Analyze app usage patterns to improve features
- Debug and fix technical issues
- Understand user engagement and app performance
- Optimize the user experience

### Authentication
- Verify your identity using Google Sign-In
- Maintain secure access to your account
- Enable data synchronization across your devices

## Data Storage and Security

### Cloud Storage
Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services:
- **Firebase Authentication**: Manages your account credentials
- **Cloud Firestore**: Stores your water intake records and settings
- **Firebase Storage**: Stores any uploaded profile images (if applicable)

### Security Measures
We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:
- All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
- Firebase security rules restrict data access to authenticated users only
- Each user can only access their own data
- Regular security updates and patches

### Data Retention
- Your data is retained as long as your account is active
- You can delete your account and all associated data at any time
- After account deletion, your data is permanently removed from our servers

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is only shared in the following circumstances:

- **With Your Consent**: When you explicitly authorize data sharing
