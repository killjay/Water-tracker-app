Privacy Policy

H2O Water Tracker

Last Updated: February 3, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how H2O Water Tracker ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application. By using H2O Water Tracker, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you use our app, we collect the following personal information:

  • Email Address: Collected when you sign in with Google to create and manage your account
  • Display Name: Optionally collected from your Google account profile
  • Profile Photo: Optionally collected from your Google account profile

Health & Fitness Data

  • Water Intake Records: Date, time, and amount of water consumed
  • Daily Goals: Your personalized daily water intake targets
  • Weight Information: Used to calculate recommended water intake goals (if provided)
  • Activity Level: Used to personalize hydration recommendations (if provided)
  • Sleep Schedule: Used to optimize reminder timing (if provided)

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version
  • App Usage Data: Features used, session duration, app interactions (via Firebase Analytics)
  • Crash Reports: Technical information about app errors to improve stability
  • Push Notification Tokens: Device tokens for sending hydration reminders (if enabled)

How We Use Your Information

App Functionality

  • Create and manage your user account
  • Store and sync your water intake data across devices
  • Calculate personalized hydration goals
  • Display your water intake history and statistics
  • Send hydration reminder notifications (if enabled)

App Improvement

  • Analyze app usage patterns to improve features
  • Debug and fix technical issues
  • Understand user engagement and app performance
  • Optimize the user experience

Authentication

  • Verify your identity using Google Sign-In
  • Maintain secure access to your account
  • Enable data synchronization across your devices

Data Storage and Security

Cloud Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services:

  • Firebase Authentication: Manages your account credentials
  • Cloud Firestore: Stores your water intake records and settings
  • Firebase Storage: Stores any uploaded profile images (if applicable)

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Firebase security rules restrict data access to authenticated users only
  • Each user can only access their own data
  • Regular security updates and patches

Data Retention

  • Your data is retained as long as your account is active
  • You can delete your account and all associated data at any time
  • After account deletion, your data is permanently removed from our servers

Third-Party Services

H2O Water Tracker uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

Google Sign-In

  • Provider: Google LLC
  • Purpose: User authentication
  • Data Shared: Email address, name, profile photo
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Firebase (Google)

  • Provider: Google LLC
  • Services Used: Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Analytics, Cloud Messaging, Storage
  • Purpose: Backend infrastructure, data storage, analytics, notifications
  • Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Firebase Analytics

  • Provider: Google LLC
  • Purpose: Analyze app usage and improve user experience
  • Data Collected: Device information, app usage patterns, crash reports
  • Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is only shared in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize data sharing
  • Service Providers: With Firebase/Google services necessary to operate the app
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Access and Control

  • View all your stored water intake data within the app
  • Update your profile information and preferences
  • Modify or delete individual water intake entries
  • Adjust your daily goals and settings

Data Portability

  • Export your water intake history from the app
  • Request a copy of your data by contacting us

Account Deletion

  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time through the app settings
  • Contact us to request account deletion

Opt-Out Options

  • Disable push notifications in app settings or device settings
  • Disable analytics tracking (note: this may limit some app functionality)
  • Sign out to stop data synchronization

Children's Privacy

H2O Water Tracker is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will delete such information.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country of residence. By using H2O Water Tracker, you consent to the transfer of your data to countries that may have different data protection laws than your country. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data.

Offline Usage

H2O Water Tracker supports offline functionality:

  • Data is cached locally on your device when offline
  • Changes are automatically synchronized when internet connection is restored
  • Local data is protected by your device's security features
