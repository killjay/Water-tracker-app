Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how H2O Water Tracker ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application. By using H2O Water Tracker, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Collection of Information

Personal Information

When you use our app, we collect the following personal information:

  • Email address (collected when you sign in with Google)
  • Display name (optionally collected from your Google account)
  • Profile photo (optionally collected from your Google account)

Health & Fitness Data

  • Water intake records (date, time, and amount)
  • Daily hydration goals
  • Weight information (if provided)
  • Activity level (if provided)
  • Sleep schedule (if provided)

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (model, operating system version)
  • App usage data (features used, session duration)
  • Crash reports
  • Push notification tokens (if enabled)

Use of Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your user account
  • Store and sync your water intake data across devices
  • Calculate personalized hydration goals
  • Display your water intake history and statistics
  • Send hydration reminder notifications (if enabled)
  • Analyze app usage patterns to improve features
  • Debug and fix technical issues
  • Verify your identity using Google Sign-In

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services (Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, and Firebase Storage). We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission using HTTPS/TLS
  • Firebase security rules that restrict data access to authenticated users only
  • Each user can only access their own data
  • Regular security updates and patches

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time, after which your data is permanently removed from our servers.

Third-Party Services

H2O Water Tracker uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In: For user authentication (email, name, profile photo). Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Firebase (Google): For backend infrastructure, data storage, analytics, and notifications. Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is only shared in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • With Firebase/Google services necessary to operate the app
  • If required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • View all your stored water intake data within the app
  • Update your profile information and preferences
  • Modify or delete individual water intake entries
  • Export your water intake history
  • Delete your account and all associated data through the app settings
  • Disable push notifications in app or device settings
  • Request a copy of your data by contacting us

Children's Privacy

H2O Water Tracker is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will delete such information.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country of residence. By using H2O Water Tracker, you consent to the transfer of your data to countries that may have different data protection laws than your country.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
